The Rule of Three

A brief introduction

There is a theory of storytelling that is called the "Rule of Three". The principle suggests that people better understand groups, concepts, and ideas when placed in groups of three. There are some famous examples of sayings that use the rule of three. They include "Life, Liberty, and the Pursuit of Happiness", "Liberté, égalité, fraternité", "Stop, Drop, and Roll", "Veni, vidi, vici", and "see no evil, hear no evil, speak no evil".
